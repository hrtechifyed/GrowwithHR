# GrowWithHR

**GrowWithHR** is an AI-powered executive advisory platform developed by HRTechify to help founders, business leaders, and HR professionals make better people decisions through intelligent assessments and explainable recommendations.

Instead of navigating complex HR regulations and organizational decisions manually, GrowWithHR analyzes a company's unique context ("Company DNA") and generates executive-ready guidance based on applicable laws, governance principles, and organizational maturity.

---

# Vision

Make strategic HR intelligence accessible to every growing organization through AI-powered, explainable, and trustworthy executive advisory.

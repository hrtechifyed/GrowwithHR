About the Founder

I built GrowItWithHR after repeatedly seeing startup founders struggle with labour law compliance, hiring decisions, workforce planning and HR processes at different stages of growth.

Many companies either discover compliance requirements too late or depend on fragmented advice from multiple consultants.

GrowItWithHR is an initiative of HRTechify that aims to simplify compliance and people operations by helping businesses understand what applies to them, when it applies and what actions they should take next.

The goal is simple: help growing companies stay compliant and scale confidently.

As of now, GrowItWithHR is a pilot initiative. I am actively seeking feedback from founders, HR leaders, consultants and ecosystem partners who want to help shape the future of compliance and people operations for growing businesses.

PRIVACY

Your assessment stays yours.

GrowWithHR is designed to provide practical people and compliance guidance while respecting your organisation's information.

Report Retention

GrowWithHR does not permanently store your assessment, organisation profile or generated advisory reports.

Reports are generated only during your current assessment session. Please download your advisory report for future reference before leaving the application.

Whenever your organisation grows, expands into new locations, or your workforce changes, simply run a new assessment to receive updated recommendations.

🔒

What We Collect

Company profile details entered during the assessment and, if you choose to download the report, your email address. Assessment information is used only during the current session and is not retained after report generation.

🛡️

How It Is Used

Your information is used only to generate your advisory report and, with your consent, to communicate GrowWithHR product updates.

🚫

What We Don't Do

We do not sell, share or disclose your organisation's information to third parties.

📄

AI Advisory

Reports are generated using AI-assisted advisory logic combined with publicly available statutory sources. Final implementation decisions should always be independently reviewed.

About the Founder

I built GrowItWithHR after repeatedly seeing startup founders struggle with labour law compliance, workforce planning, hiring decisions and people operations at different stages of growth.

Many businesses discover compliance obligations too late or depend on fragmented information from multiple advisors.

GrowItWithHR is an initiative of HRTechify aimed at making compliance and HR decision-making simpler, clearer and more actionable.

The goal is simple: help growing companies stay compliant and scale confidently.

As of now, GrowItWithHR is a pilot initiative and I am actively seeking feedback from founders, HR leaders, consultants and ecosystem partners.

About the Founder

I have built GrowItWithHR after repeatedly seeing startup founders struggle with labour law compliance, hiring decisions, workforce planning and HR processes at different stages of growth.

Many companies either discover compliance requirements too late or depend on fragmented advice from multiple consultants.

GrowItWithHR is an attempt of HRTechify that aims to simplify compliance and people operations by helping businesses understand what applies to them, when it applies and what actions they should take next.

The goal is simple: help growing companies stay compliant and scale confidently.

As of now this is a pilot initiative and I am looking for enthusiastic partners to work along me on taking the concept to the next level and scale it
